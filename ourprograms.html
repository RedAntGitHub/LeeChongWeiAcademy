LCW Badminton Camp 2019

Intensive badminton training for 4 days-3 nights.

Register Now

Camp Brief and Objectives

Wholly designed and conducted by Academy Head Coach, Coach Chang – Former Malaysia National Men’s Double Player and National Coach.

Nurture badminton skills among badminton lovers during school holidays.

The camp focuses on improving campers' badminton skills while emphasising offensive strategies, defensive tactics, and game fundamentals.

All ages and levels are welcome and campers are separated accordingly.

Camp Activities

Star Trainers

Players are invited to join the ranks of former professional shuttlers such as Chang Kim Wai, Mooi Hing Yau and a mystery national players to be invited to camp.

Tricks & Techniques

Apart from training on basic techniques, shuttlecock trajectory will be also analysed in order to train players to defend calmly and attack proactively.

Boosting Fitness

A series of rigorous physical training will be implemented to strengthen participant’s physical endurance, at the same time developing their mental perseverance!

Joyful Learning

The module is made enjoyable with interesting and diverse learning methods, which help to induce motivation, allowing participants to play, learn and grow in a pleasurable environment.

Friendship Forever

Participants from all over the world including Singapore, Hong Kong, China, India and other countries are gathered together in exchange of culture, skills and beautiful memories!

Award and Reorganization

Players will be evaluated by their skills, discipline, performance and etc over the 4 days stay. The hour before the camp dismiss, player will be invited to Closing Ceremony to receive their award and recognition as a token of encouragement.

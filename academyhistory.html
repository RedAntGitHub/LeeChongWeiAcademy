Academy History (City of Origins)

Humble Beginnings

Born in Perak, Lee Chong Wei preferred basketball in his early years. At the age of 11, his father brought him to a badminton hall and his talent was discovered by a coach. After receiving his father's consent, the coach began to train Lee after school and from there onwards, he fell in love with badminton and the rest is history.

To give back to the state that made him who he was, Lee Chong Wei picked Perak to be the starting point of his dream of cultivating the love of badminton in the entire nation. As a city known for its food, mountains and valleys, Ipoh is naturally a good starting point.
